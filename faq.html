How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. Go to a printer at hz.
  2. Log in with your hz username and password.
  3. Place your paper on the feeder or glass plate.
  4. Press start
  5. Log off from the printer.

How can you book a project space?
  1. Go to the home page of the Self Service Portal
  2. Click on reservations
  3. Choose the appropriate category and search for the "resource" you want to reserve. If necessary, use the Type filter to turn certain items on/off in the overview.
  4. Select the day when the resource is borrowed
  5. By dragging the left mouse button, indicate the time period when the resource is borrowed
  6. Click Next
  7. Read the loan conditions, check the reservation and click Next
  8. Fill in the necessary information and click Submit request

For more information click here

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You need to park your car on Parkeergarage Kousteensedijk, Middelburg. You can grab a ticket from the reception office on the HZ location Middelburg. Stick the ticket into the machine when you drive out and everything is free!
