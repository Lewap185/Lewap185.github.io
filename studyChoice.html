Why did you chose ICT?

I got my first introduction to programming in my first year on TUDelft studying mechanical engineering I chose to switch to Computer Science and Engineering also on TUDelft I programmed enough in those 2 years that I am certain I want to continue it as a career.

Why are you certain you want to continue it as a career?

ICT has good career opportunities. I am also excellent at abstract and algorithmic thinking and I also love to solve problemens related to math and computer science.

Why do you love to think algorithmicly and abstractly?

Because there is no room for ambiguity. Everything is either true or false with no in between. I personally do not like it if there is no structure or ambiguity. It gives me anxiety and no peace

Why is having good career opportunities important to you?

Having a good income is important to me because it allows me to take better care of the people around me. It gives me a sense of importance and fullfilment.

Why does it give you a sense of importance and fullfilment?

I will be able to accomplish my God-given goal better on this earth for His sake and for my family. I have a responsibility to make the most of my life and talents and I think the ICT program will allow me to accomplish that goal in the most efficient way possible.
